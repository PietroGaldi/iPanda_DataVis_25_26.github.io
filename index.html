From consumption to emissions: how our energy choices shape the planet

The relationship between energy, electricity, and CO₂ emissions

iPanda: Francesco Favareto, Pietro Galdi, Pietro Panizza, Mirco Senes

This project aims to visualize different informations regarding energy use and production and how it affects the environment. There will be some focus on CO2 emissions and the general state of our planet. To do this, we will use data from four different dataset:

  • CO2 emissions per capita
  • Energy use per person
  • Global primary energy consumption by source
  • Electricity production by source

We're going to use the datasets from ourworldindata.org, section Energy and Environment
